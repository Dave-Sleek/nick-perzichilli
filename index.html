I'm Nick Perzichilli — an Entrepreneur, Venture Strategist, and Marketing Executive.

Nick Perzichilli is a seasoned entrepreneur with expertise spanning marketing, e-commerce, banking, and Web3. His entrepreneurial acumen shines with companies he co-founded reaching big exits, including a pivotal role in the $300 million acquisition by American Express.

Today, he spearheads AdvisorX, a premier 300+ team of Web3 advisors and experts offering capital, treasury management, market making, advisory, GTM, TGE, community & growth services. Humble and grateful for his achievements, Nick looks forward to brightening the Web3 revolution.
